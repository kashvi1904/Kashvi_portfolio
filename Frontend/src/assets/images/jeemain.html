JEE Main 2021 : Applications for February exams closes today - JEE Mains Complete Syllabus is on NTA official site

Central-Jobs, exam results, Exam-Results, IIT JEE Application, IIT JEE Mains, IIT JEE Mains 2021, IIT JEE Syllabus, IIT-JEE,

JEE Main 2021 Applications for February exams closes today:



For JEE Mains, National Testing Agency(NTA) released complete syllabus on jeemain.nta.nic.in .We have provided complete syllabus link below, Candidates can check the link to know th syllabus. Applications for February 2021 exams closes today(Jan 23rd 2021).


As there was some confusion regarding the syllabus for the JEE examination, NTA has released the complete syllabus. The JEE Mains 2021 Complete Syllabus PDF can be downloaded from here. Just to clarify, candidates who have registered for the exam are advised to note that the syllabus has not been revised. This year also it will be to remains the same syllabus for competitive examination. However, with the revised syllabus for CBSE and many state boards, NTA would this year instead provide choice to candidates.


whatever the syllabus that has been removed would be covered in the choice sections. For JEE Main 2021 exam, a 50 per cent choice would be provided in numerical section of the BE/ BTech paper. Students should attempt 15 out of the 30 questions provided in each Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics section.


Candidates who want to appear for the JEE Main 2021 February examinations are required to register and complete the online applications by January 23, 2021. Candidates can apply on jeemain.nta.nic.in. As for the application fees, the window to pay online fee would remain open till 11:50 pm on January 24, 2021. 


For  March, April and May examinations, the registrations would be opened again later after the result declaration of Feb exam. Candidates would have an option of registering at the later date. Simillarly, students who register now would have an option of withdrawing their application when the portal reopened for any of the exams.
